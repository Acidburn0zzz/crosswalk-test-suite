Pre-condition

  • The DOMLauncher app has been installed on device
  • There has been installed a SIM card with missed calls and SMS

Test Steps

  1. Click home button in device and select DOMLauncher
  2. Check the missed calls and SMS in the bottom page
  3. Select and launch an app
  4. Click '<menu>' --> '<system>', then click on the right content
  5. Setting 'Fullscreen' '<off>' to <on>
  6. Setting 'Orientation' '<rotate>' to <landscape> or <portrait>
  7. Setting 'Mobile Data' '<off>', then open one webpage via browser
  8. Setting 'Mobile Data' '<off>' to <on>, then open one webpage via browser
  9. Setting 'Wifi' '<off>' to <on>, then check the bottom page
  10. Setting 'Ringer Volume' number increase or decrease
  11. Setting 'Ringer Mode' '<normal>' to <silent> or <vibrate>
  12. Setting 'Media Volume' number increase or decrease
  13. Setting 'Bluetooth' '<off>' to <on>
  14. Setting 'Brightness' number increase or decrease
  15. Setting 'Auto-Brightness' '<off>' to <on>
  16. Setting the phone mode to flight mode, then check the bottom page

Expected Results

  1. Launch the DOMLauncher app successfully, no error
  2. Display <calls-<num>> and <sms-<num>>
  3. Launch the app successfully, no error
  4. Display the detailed information about system setting, such as 'Fullscreen', 'Wifi' and so on
  5. Hide the Status Bar
  6. Display the device content with landscape screen or portrait screen
  7. Can not open the webpage
  8. Open the webpage successfully, no error
  9. Display <wifi-<num>%> in the bottom page
  10. The volume bigger or smaller
  11. Display ringer mode silent or vibrate in Status Bar
  12. The volume bigger or smaller
  13. Display bluetooth enable connection in Status Bar
  14. Increase or decrease the screen brightness
  15. Enable the auto-brightness screen mode
  16. The <signal-<num>%> changed <signal-0%>
